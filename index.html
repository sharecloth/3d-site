Empowering innovators in fashion 3D-printing

ShareCloth is introducing a desktop editor for clothing 3D-printing. Bridging the gap between fashion design and 3D graphic design, ShareCloth helps fashion designers fit, customize, and print garments by using regular digital patterns.

How does it work?

ShareCloth Editor allows fashion designers to connect their knowledge of garment design with revolutionary 3D-printing technology

User garment pattern

You can print any garment. All you need is a digital or digitized pattern! Don’t have one? Order or download one for free today!

Customize your design

Use cutting and design tools to make quick fixes and customize your garment

Fit it

Check your design fit on a virtual model or body scan

Print it

Print your 3D garments just like a word document!

Wear

Change the world by changing your self and wearing sustainable and biodegradable garments you've made by yourself

Re-use

Create your own digital pattern library, make projects and fabric presets to streamline creating new styles

Features

  • Easy-to-use application for Mac and Windows
  • Turn flat digital patterns into a 3D printed garment hassle-free
  • Customize your fit and design with ease
  • Fit made garments on a virtual model or body scan
  • Applicable with a 3D printer
  • Use material filament presets for precise garment simulation
