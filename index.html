How it works?

ShareCloth Editor allows fashion designer to connect knowledge in design and cutting
with revolutionary 3D-printing technology

User garment pattern

You can print any garment having only its digital or digitized pattern. Don't have one? Order it or download for free

Customize design

Use cutting and design tools to make fast fixes and customize your garment

Fit it

Check your design fit on a virtual model or a body scan.

Print it

Remember how to print document? Print your garment in the similar way

Share

Share your precise garment 3D sample with your clients, friends of ShareCloth' community

Re-use

Create your own digital library of patterns, made projects and fabric presets to simplify new creation

Features

  • Easy-to-use application for Mac and Windows
  • Turn flat digital pattern into a 3D printed garment with no pain
  • Customize the fit and design with ease
  • Fit made garment on the virtual model or body scan
  • Applicable with your home 3D printer
  • Use material filament presets for precise garment simulation
